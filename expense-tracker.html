Get Started — 4 Simple Steps
Follow these steps each time you want to import a month of bank transactions. Takes about 5 minutes once you've done it once.
1
Set up your categories & profile names
In Settings, enter names for each person using this tool, confirm your currency, and review the default expense categories. You can add, rename or recolour them. Click Save when done — the AI prompt below will update automatically to match.
2
Download your bank statement
Log in to your banking app or website and download your statement for the month you want to import. Excel or CSV format is strongly preferred over PDF — PDFs are sometimes misread by AI. Most banks offer both options. Download one month at a time.
💡 Tip: If your bank only offers PDF, that usually works fine — just double-check the imported rows afterwards for any misread amounts.
3
Use AI to categorize your transactions
Open any AI assistant that supports file uploads — Claude, ChatGPT Plus, or Gemini Advanced all work well. Copy the prompt below, paste it into the chat, attach your bank statement file, and send. The AI will return a ready-to-import CSV file.

⚠ AI returned text instead of a file?

            
Some free AI plans display the CSV as text in the chat instead of generating a file. That's fine — just select all the CSV text in the reply, copy it, paste it into a blank Notepad or TextEdit file, and save it with a .csv extension (e.g. january.csv). Then import that file here normally.
